ABOUT ME

I’m Angelica Morales, and I am a designer from Mexico, with a passion for interaction and design.

I have collaborated on various projects for well-known clients, including Samsung, Coca-Cola, and TED Education. Additionally, I have contributed to several award-winning animated projects and have experience in commercial voice acting.

Experience

Angelica Morales is a Design Researcher with 5+ years in UX design and 3 years in 3D design and illustration. She combines experience in animation and UX to create impactful, user-centered solutions across industries like banking, entertainment, mental health, and retail.

She specializes in behavioral design, AI integration, and conducting usability tests, user interviews, and expert evaluations. Additionally, she provides consulting services to top e-commerce platforms.
